# AI-Hand-gesture-game
Creating an AI gesture game.
We used the Avishkar ( https://ams.avishkaar.cc/ ) AI hand gesture game builder feature to create our game.
Game description: We have created a sprite named 'Chintu' who is a cute little alien. And we have 2 more sprites that are 'Meteorites' attacking our 'Chintu'. You must enable your camera for the gesture detection. Our cute chintu is gonna be attached to your index finger tip. You must move 'Chintu' with your finger and try to keep him away from the attacking meteorites for as long as you can. Your score will be calculated as (Score = Number of seconds chintu was safe ).
